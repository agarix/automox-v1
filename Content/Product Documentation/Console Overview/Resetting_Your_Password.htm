Resetting Your Password

If you forget your password, you can reset it from the console login page. https://console.automox.com/

  1. Enter your email address in the login window. 
  2. Click: Forgot Password
  3. Enter your email address to receive a reset link.
  4. Click Send Reset Link.

Password Requirements

Password requirements are as follows:

  • Must be at least 12 characters in length, but no more than 64
  • Must contain at least one lowercase and one uppercase letter
  • Must contain at least one number
  • Must contain at least one special character: ~ ! @ # $ % ^ & * _ - + = ` \ | ( ) [ ] : ; " ' < > , . ? /
  • Passwords (new or updated) are screened against lists of commonly used or compromised passwords
