Allowing Automox Notifications on macOS When Screen Sharing

The macOS Notifications Center requires an extra step to allow Automox Notifications to appear during screen sharing sessions.

To allow notifications during shared sessions, do the following:

  1. On your computer, go to System Settings and select Notifications.
  2. Switch on the toggle for Allow notifications when mirroring or sharing the display

This will ensure that notifications appear in the top-right corner of your screen when mirroring or sharing.
